## Real rupiah anchors pulled from research C (asset-math)

- Rokok Rp25rb/hari ≈ Rp9 juta/tahun ≈ 7 kambing muda (modul 2 & 4).
- Tabungan 10% dari gaji Rp2,5jt = Rp250rb (modul 3).
- Reksadana Pasar Uang & Tabungan Emas mulai Rp10.000; RDPU ~4–6%/thn vs tabungan 0,5–1,5% (modul 3, 5, 8).
- Dana darurat: target awal Rp1 juta → 3–6 bulan biaya hidup (modul 5).
- Kambing: 2 betina muda ~Rp2,4 juta → ~20 ekor / Rp30–40 juta dalam 5 tahun; pakan ngarit ~Rp40rb/ekor/bln (modul 7, 8).
- Warung modal Rp5–15 juta, untung bersih ~Rp7 juta/bln (modul 8).
- Kos 4 kamar (tanah sendiri) ~Rp2,7 juta/bln (modul 8).
- Reksadana Rp300rb/bln × 15 thn @ ~6% ≈ Rp87 juta dari ~Rp54 juta setoran (modul 7).
- Emas: naik ~4x dalam 10 tahun; beli LM bersertifikat bukan perhiasan (modul 8).

## Pedagogy rules followed (research A & B)

- One concept per lesson; 2–4 min reading; ≤~300 words body.
- Every lesson ends in one verb-first, binary, do-it-this-week action.
- Every abstract term (reksadana, inflasi, bunga berbunga, leverage) taught through a tangible picture and immediately explained in plain words.
- Reader addressed as "kamu". No individual named. No startup/SaaS/stock-picking framing.
- Honest about risk (penyakit kambing, spread emas, reksadana saham bisa rugi, warung = kerja keras 12 jam, kos butuh modal besar).
