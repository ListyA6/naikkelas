# C — Asset Math: Real Indonesian Unit Economics for Small-Scale Asset Building

**Audience:** SMA-educated restaurant workers in Kediri, East Java.
**Goal of curriculum:** Reach financial freedom by ~age 40 through tangible, believable, small-scale assets — NOT startups, NOT stock-picking lottery tickets.
**Rule for these numbers:** every figure here is something a warung cook in Kediri would nod at, not laugh at. Prices are recent (2025–early 2026), sourced inline. Currency is rupiah (Rp). All worked examples state their assumptions and risks honestly.

> **Reality check before we start:** No asset here makes you rich fast. The whole game is *slow, boring, repeated* — buy a thing that earns or grows, let time do the work, never sell the seed corn. A worker who saves Rp500rb/month and parks it in the right place for 15 years beats almost everyone who chases the "cuan cepat" dream.

## 1. KAMBING / GOAT FARMING — the flagship "compounding" example

This is the curriculum's anchor because a goat is the most *visible* compounding machine a villager knows: 2 goats become 5, become 10, become 20. You can touch it. Your grandfather did it.

### 1.1 Buy price (East Java, 2025–2026)

Goats are priced by sex, age, and weight. The cheapest entry is a **cempe** (kid) or young doe.

| Type | Price range |
|---|---|
| Kambing Jawa **betina cempe** (female kid) | **Rp600.000 – Rp750.000** |
| Kambing Jawa **betina muda** (young doe) | **Rp1.200.000 – Rp1.250.000** |
| Kambing Jawa **betina dewasa** (adult doe) | Rp1.500.000 – Rp1.550.000 |
| Kambing Jawa **jantan dewasa** (adult buck, for breeding) | Rp1.600.000 – Rp2.400.000 |
| Kambing Bligon betina dewasa | Rp1.750.000 – Rp1.850.000 |

Sources: [Suara — Daftar Harga Kambing Kurban 2026](https://www.suara.com/news/2026/05/05/204217/daftar-harga-kambing-kurban-2026-terbaru-mulai-rp1-jutaan-cek-rekomendasinya-di-sini), [Aqiqahberkah — Daftar Harga Kambing Jawa](https://www.aqiqahberkah.com/daftar-harga-kambing-jawa-2022), [Kontan — Harga kambing & sapi kurban Jatim 2025](https://regional.kontan.co.id/news/cek-harga-kambing-dan-sapi-untuk-kurban-2025-wilayah-jawa-timur-berikut-daftarnya).

**For the example we buy young does (betina muda) at ~Rp1,2jt each** — old enough to breed within months, cheaper than a full adult.

### 1.2 Breeding cycle (the engine)

- **Gestation (masa bunting):** ~5 months / 150 days. ([DKPP Jabar](https://www.facebook.com/dkpp.jawabarat/posts/1196998592249135/))
- **Kidding interval:** 7–8 months between births → **a doe can give birth 3 times in 2 years** (≈1.5 litters/year). ([DKPP Jabar](https://www.facebook.com/dkpp.jawabarat/posts/1196998592249135/))
- **Kids per birth:** usually 1–2 (twins common in kambing Jawa/Kacang); we model a *conservative* average of **1.5 surviving kids per birth**.
- **Maturity / breeding age:** a doe is ready to breed at ~8–12 months; a kid reaches sellable/qurban size in ~12–18 months.

### 1.3 Mortality & feed cost

- **Mortality:** a goat operation is considered healthy if deaths stay **under 10%**. Kids (cempe) are the most fragile. We model **15% loss** to be honest, not optimistic. ([Unram reproduction study](https://unram.sgp1.digitaloceanspaces.com/simlitabmas/kinerja/penelitian/jurnal/163345e9-c456-4ba0-9e6f-0d0eebc6137f-ARTIKEL%20PENELITIAN%202024.pdf))
- **Feed:** a goat eats ~10% of body weight in greens daily. For a *kampung* keeper who cuts grass himself (**ngarit**), the green feed is effectively **free labour, not cash** — ngarit is "pekerjaan sambilan," a side chore. Cash cost is only supplemental concentrate (dedak/bekatul ~Rp3.000/kg). ([Agropustaka](https://www.agropustaka.id/kabar/tips-pemberian-pakan-kambing-dan-domba/), [BBPP Kupang](https://bbppkupang.bppsdmp.pertanian.go.id/blog/pemberian-pakan-kambing-yang-sesuai-dengan-umurnya))

**Realistic cash feed cost:** ~Rp30.000–Rp50.000 per goat per month *if* you ngarit (cut your own grass) and only buy a little concentrate. If you buy ALL feed, it jumps to ~Rp150.000–Rp250.000/goat/month and the math gets much worse. **This model assumes the ngarit case** — which is exactly how a Kediri villager would actually do it.

### 1.4 Sell price — the Idul Adha / qurban premium

The whole trick of goat farming is *timing the sale to qurban season*. The same goat sells for a big premium right before Idul Adha.

- Normal adult goat: ~Rp1,5jt–Rp1,7jt.
- **Qurban-ready goat (Jatim, 2025):** starts at **Rp2.500.000**, range **Rp1,1jt–Rp2,9jt** for 2026, premium animals **Rp4jt**. ([Kontan](https://regional.kontan.co.id/news/cek-harga-sapi-dan-kambing-untuk-idul-adha-2025-wilayah-jawa-timur-mulai-rp-2-jutaan), [Suara](https://www.suara.com/news/2026/05/05/204217/daftar-harga-kambing-kurban-2026-terbaru-mulai-rp1-jutaan-cek-rekomendasinya-di-sini))

**Takeaway price for the model: a healthy 12–18 month goat sells for ~Rp2.000.000 at qurban season.**

### 1.5 Worked example: 2 does → ~20 goats over ~5 years

**Assumptions (kept deliberately conservative):**
- Start: **2 young does** + share/rent a buck (or pay ~Rp100rb stud fee per mating). Start cost ≈ 2 × Rp1,2jt = **Rp2.400.000**.
- Each doe produces **1.5 surviving kids per birth**, **1.5 births/year** → ~2.25 surviving kids per doe per year, BUT we apply **15% mortality** and round DOWN hard for realism.
- Cash feed cost: ~Rp40rb/goat/month (ngarit case).
- We **sell some kids each qurban season** to cover feed + take small cash, and **keep the female kids to grow the herd**.

| End of year | Breeding does | Total herd (does + growing kids) | Kids born (net of death) | Sold at qurban | Cash from sales | Feed cost (yr, avg herd) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Start | 2 | 2 | — | — | — | — |
| Year 1 | 2 | ~4 | ~3 | 1 | ~Rp2,0jt | ~Rp1,9jt |
| Year 2 | 3 | ~6 | ~4 | 2 | ~Rp4,0jt | ~Rp2,9jt |
| Year 3 | 4 | ~10 | ~6 | 3 | ~Rp6,0jt | ~Rp4,8jt |
| Year 4 | 6 | ~15 | ~9 | 4 | ~Rp8,0jt | ~Rp7,2jt |
| Year 5 | 8 | **~20** | ~12 | 6 | ~Rp12,0jt | ~Rp9,6jt |

**Reading the table honestly:**
- The herd really does compound **2 → 4 → 6 → 10 → 15 → 20** in about 5 years if you keep the females and only sell the surplus males/extras.
- **Cash in years 1–3 is thin** — most of the "profit" is *more goats*, not money in your pocket. Feed cost roughly eats the sale cash early on. This is the honest part most "ternak cuan" posts hide.
- The real payoff is the **asset value of the herd**: by year 5, ~20 goats at ~Rp1,5jt–Rp2jt each ≈ **Rp30jt–Rp40jt of standing wealth**, built from a Rp2,4jt start, mostly by cutting grass.
- From year 4–5 onward, qurban sales of Rp8jt–Rp12jt/year become real annual income while the herd still holds at 15–20.

**Risks to teach loudly:**
1. **Disease wipeouts** (PMK/scabies/worms) can kill half a herd in weeks — the #1 real-world risk. Vaccination + clean pen is non-negotiable.
2. **Mortality on kids** is higher than people admit; one bad rainy season hurts.
3. **It's labour, not passive** — ngarit is daily work. If you have to *buy* all feed, the profit largely disappears.
4. **Price risk** — if everyone sells at qurban, local price can dip. Don't bet the whole herd on one Idul Adha.

**Plain-language takeaway:** *A goat is a savings account that eats grass and makes copies of itself. Start with 2, never sell the females, cut your own grass, sell the extras at qurban. In 5 years two goats quietly become a herd worth more than a year of your salary — but it's real work and disease can hurt you, so keep the pen clean and don't get greedy.*

## 2. WARUNG / FOOD STALL — unit economics in a small city

The asset a restaurant worker understands best: they already work in one.

### 2.1 Startup capital

- **Simple warung makan / nasi (home-based or small kiosk):** **Rp5jt – Rp15jt** for basic kitchen gear, gerobak/tables, initial stock.
- **Warung sembako (grocery), small scale:** Rp10jt – Rp25jt (more inventory-heavy).
Sources: [Seva — Usaha warung makan modal berapa 2025](https://www.seva.id/blog/usaha-warung-makan-modal-berapa-cek-hitungan-biaya-peralatan-dan-potensi-cuan-di-2025-kg), [UKM Indonesia — warung sembako](https://ukmindonesia.id/baca-deskripsi-posts/bisnis-warung-sembako-perhitungan-modal-untung-dan-balik-modalnya).

### 2.2 Revenue & profit ranges

These scale entirely with foot traffic. Honest spread:

| Scenario | Customers/day × price | Monthly omzet | Net profit/month (after food cost, gas, etc.) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Small / quiet warung | 30 × Rp15rb | ~Rp13,5jt | ~Rp3jt – Rp5jt |
| Solid neighborhood warung | 50 × Rp20rb | ~Rp30jt | ~Rp6,8jt – Rp15jt |
| Busy warung nasi | 100 × Rp25rb | ~Rp45jt – Rp75jt | ~Rp10jt – Rp20jt |

Sources: [Liputan6 — skema modal warung nasi 2025](https://www.liputan6.com/hot/read/6178523/skema-modal-usaha-warung-nasi-di-rumah-peluang-cuan-di-2025), [Seva](https://www.seva.id/blog/usaha-warung-makan-modal-berapa-cek-hitungan-biaya-peralatan-dan-potensi-cuan-di-2025-kg).

> Net margin on warung makan typically lands around **15–30%** of omzet once you pay for ingredients, gas, and (if any) rent. The numbers above assume the owner works in it — paying a full cook eats the profit.

### 2.3 Worked example & payback

- Startup: **Rp10jt**.
- Omzet: 50 customers × Rp20rb × 30 days = **Rp30jt/month**.
- Costs: ingredients (~40%) + gas + small rent + bumbu = ~Rp23jt.
- **Net ≈ Rp7jt/month.**
- **Payback ≈ Rp10jt ÷ Rp7jt ≈ 1.5 months** *if it's busy from day one* — but realistically allow **3–6 months** to build a regular crowd.

**Risks:** location is everything (a quiet spot can lose money for months), ingredient/food waste, and burnout — it's 12-hour days. A warung is a *job that owns a small asset*, not passive income.

**Plain-language takeaway:** *A warung pays back its small startup fast IF the spot is good, but it's hard daily work and the profit lives in the margin. Treat it as buying yourself a job with upside — and pour the profit into the truly passive assets below (gold, reksadana, goats, kos) so you eventually don't NEED to stand at the stove.*

## 3. KOS-KOSAN / BOARDING ROOMS — rent income in Kediri

The dream asset: build rooms once, collect rent monthly. Real, but capital-heavy, so it's a *later-stage* goal in the curriculum.

### 3.1 Cost to build a room (Kediri)

- **Per room, standard quality:** **Rp30jt – Rp65jt** (avg often quoted ~Rp50jt for a room with private bathroom).
- **Construction in Kediri:** **Rp2,5jt – Rp4jt per m²**.
Sources: [Kediri Properti — anggaran bangun rumah kost Kediri](https://www.kediriproperti.id/2025/10/anggaran-bangun-rumah-kost-kediri.html), [Seva — biaya membangun kost 20 kamar 2025](https://www.seva.id/blog/berapa-biaya-membangun-kost-20-kamar-di-2025-ini-rincian-lengkap-per-kamar-dan-material-kg), [Finansialku — bisnis kos 10 kamar](https://www.finansialku.com/properti/bisnis-kos-kosan/).
**This excludes land** — land is the big hidden cost. The example below assumes you already own the land (e.g. family plot), which is the realistic path for a local.

### 3.2 Rent, occupancy, yield

- **Rent per room (kamar mandi dalam, listrik, Wi-Fi):** **Rp750rb – Rp1jt/month**.
- **Occupancy:** model **85%** (strong near campus/market). ([Kediri Properti investasi](https://www.kediriproperti.id/2025/10/panduan-investasi-rumah-kost-kediri.html))
- **Rule-of-thumb yield:** **6–8%/year** on the *building* cost; **18–23%/year** if land appreciation is counted. ([Kediri Properti](https://www.kediriproperti.id/2025/10/panduan-investasi-rumah-kost-kediri.html))

### 3.3 Worked example — start small, 4 rooms

You don't build 20 rooms. You build **4** behind your house.

- Build cost: 4 × Rp45jt = **Rp180jt** (land already owned).
- Rent: Rp800rb/room × 4 × 85% occupancy = **~Rp2,72jt/month** = **~Rp32,6jt/year**.
- Minus maintenance/electricity/repairs (~15%) → **~Rp27,7jt/year net**.
- **Gross yield ≈ Rp32,6jt ÷ Rp180jt ≈ 18%/year** on rent alone (because land was free to you).
- **Payback on the building ≈ Rp180jt ÷ Rp27,7jt ≈ ~6,5 years**, after which it's near-pure income for decades, plus the land keeps rising.

**Risks:** big up-front capital (this is a *graduation* asset, not a starter), vacancy in a bad location, tenant damage/late payment, and you must already have or cheaply acquire land — buying land changes the math completely.

**Plain-language takeaway:** *Kos rooms are the closest thing to a money-printing machine a normal person can build — but only after you have land and a lump of capital. Build it with money the goats, warung, gold and reksadana made you. Start with 2–4 rooms, not 20. Once paid back in ~6–7 years, it feeds you for life.*

## 4. EMAS / LOGAM MULIA — gold as the beginner's store of value

Gold is the curriculum's **default first asset** because: it never goes to zero, you can start tiny, and every Indonesian already trusts it.

### 4.1 Price history (Antam, per gram, IDR)

| Year | Antam ~price/gram |
|---|---|
| 2015 | Rp589.000 |
| 2018 | Rp651.000 |
| 2020 | Rp781.000 |
| 2021 | Rp940.000 |
| 2023 | Rp1.017.000 |
| 2024 | Rp1.136.000 |
| 2025 | ~Rp1.660.000 |
| Jan 2026 (record) | **Rp3.168.000** |

Sources: [GoodStats — harga emas Antam melonjak 2026](https://goodstats.id/article/harga-emas-antam-melonjak-awal-2026-tertinggi-dalam-10-tahun-m3d58), [Databoks/Katadata — kenaikan 10 tahun](https://databoks.katadata.co.id/en/market/statistics/68d2424cc1aca/antam-gold-price-increase-over-the-last-10-years-september-23-2025), [Kompas — harga emas Antam 5 Juni 2026](https://money.kompas.com/read/2026/06/05/085438226/harga-emas-antam-hari-ini-5-juni-2026-naik-rp-11000-per-gram-cek-daftar).

- **10-year growth (Sep 2015 → Sep 2025): +267%** — roughly **4×**, about **~13–14%/year average** in rupiah. ([Databoks](https://databoks.katadata.co.id/en/market/statistics/68d2424cc1aca/antam-gold-price-increase-over-the-last-10-years-september-23-2025))
- Note: 2024→2026 was an *unusually steep* run. Don't promise 14%/year forever — gold has flat years too. Long-run, it reliably beats inflation and protects savings; that's its job, not getting rich quick.

### 4.2 How small you can start

- **Tabungan Emas (Pegadaian):** buy from **Rp10.000**, minimum **0,01 gram**. Admin ~Rp10rb + storage ~Rp30rb/year. 24-karat backed. ([Pegadaian Tabungan Emas](https://pegadaian.co.id/produk/tabungan-emas))
- **Physical Antam 0,5 g:** ~**Rp1,46jt** sell / ~Rp1,26jt buyback (Jun 2026) — note the **buy/sell spread**: you lose ~12–15% if you sell immediately, so gold is a *hold-for-years* asset. ([LogamSpot](https://logamspot.id/harga/emas-antam-0-5-gram))

### 4.3 Worked example

A worker saves **Rp200.000/month into Tabungan Emas** (skip ~2 packs of cigarettes/week):
- That's ~Rp2,4jt/year of gold bought steadily (cicil/menabung — averages out price swings).
- At a *conservative* ~8–10%/year long-run gold appreciation, **Rp200rb/month for 10 years ≈ Rp36jt–Rp40jt** of accumulated gold value (vs ~Rp24jt put in).
- The point isn't the exact number — it's that the savings **didn't get eaten by inflation or spent**.

**Risks:** buy/sell spread (don't trade it short-term), gold can go *sideways for years*, and physical theft. Tabungan Emas removes theft risk and the tiny-start barrier — that's why it's the recommended on-ramp.

**Plain-language takeaway:** *Gold is the "can't-screw-it-up" first asset. Put Rp10rb–Rp200rb in Tabungan Emas every payday, never touch it, and in 10 years you have a real pile that held its value while rupiah inflation ate everyone who kept cash under the mattress. Slow and certain beats fast and risky.*

## 5. REKSADANA / MUTUAL FUNDS — the easiest passive growth

Reksadana lets a worker own a slice of bonds/stocks managed by professionals, starting from **Rp10.000**. Factual, not a sales pitch — returns are *not guaranteed*.

### 5.1 Typical historical returns (2025, indicative — past performance ≠ future)

| Fund type | What it holds | Risk | 2025 indicative return |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Pasar Uang** (money market) | deposits, short bonds | very low | **~4,4–4,6%/yr net** |
| **Pendapatan Tetap** (fixed income) | govt/corp bonds | low–medium | **~6,5%/yr** (best products ~12%) |
| **Saham** (equity) | stocks | high (swings, can lose) | **~22%/yr** in a good year — but can be *negative* in bad years |

Sources: [Bibit blog — 2025 recap](https://blog.bibit.id/blog-1/year-in-review-2025-key-events-and-learnings-for-2026), [Bareksa — reksadana terbaik 2025](https://www.bareksa.com/berita/reksa-dana/2025-12-31/reksadana-terbaik-2025-berdasarkan-return-mana-yang-paling-untung), [Bareksa — pendapatan tetap & pasar uang 2025](https://www.bareksa.com/berita/bareksa-insight/2025-01-13/daftar-reksadana-pendapatan-tetap-dan-pasar-uang-terbaik-pilihan-investasi-2025).

> **Honesty rule for the module:** the 22% saham number is a *good year*. Equity reksadana can drop 10–20% in a bad year. For a beginner with no buffer, **pasar uang and pendapatan tetap (4–7%/yr, very stable)** are the right starting point — think of them as a "better-than-bank-deposit" parking spot, not a casino.

### 5.2 How to start

- **Minimum: Rp10.000** to buy a unit. ([Bibit FAQ](https://faq.bibit.id/id/category/reksa-dana-1scgo7k/))
- **Platforms:** **Bibit** (beginner-friendly, auto profile-based) and **Bareksa** — both OJK-registered. (Stated factually; not an endorsement. Always check OJK registration.)
- Money is liquid: pasar uang funds can usually be withdrawn within a couple of business days — unlike a goat or a gold bar, you can get cash quickly.

### 5.3 Worked example

Worker auto-invests **Rp300.000/month into a pasar uang + pendapatan tetap mix** averaging a conservative **~6%/year**:
- After **5 years:** ~Rp18jt in, grows to **~Rp21jt**.
- After **15 years (the age-25→40 horizon):** ~Rp54jt in, grows to **~Rp87jt** — the extra ~Rp33jt is pure compounding the worker did nothing for.
- Push it to Rp500rb/month for 15 years at 6% → **~Rp145jt** from ~Rp90jt deposited.

**Risks:** returns vary year to year and are **not guaranteed**; saham funds can lose money short-term; only use OJK-registered platforms to avoid scams ("reksadana bodong"). Pasar uang is the low-drama default.

**Plain-language takeaway:** *Reksadana is gold's modern cousin — start at Rp10rb, set an auto-debit every payday, leave it alone. Stick to pasar uang / pendapatan tetap as a beginner (steady ~4–7%, sleep-at-night), and let compounding turn small monthly habits into tens of millions by age 40. It's liquid, so it's also your emergency buffer.*

## Putting it together — the believable path to ~age 40

A Kediri restaurant worker, age 25, doesn't pick ONE of these. The curriculum stacks them in order of capital required:

1. **Start (Rp10rb–Rp300rb/month):** Tabungan Emas + pasar uang reksadana. Zero barrier, builds the saving *habit* and an emergency buffer. (Sections 4 & 5)
2. **Add (Rp2,4jt one-time + grass):** 2 does. Compounds into a Rp30jt+ herd over 5 years, mostly from labour you already have. (Section 1)
3. **Level up (Rp5jt–Rp15jt):** a warung — buys you a higher-income job whose *profit* feeds 1, 2, and 4. (Section 2)
4. **Graduate (capital + land):** kos-kosan, 2–4 rooms, built from everything above. Becomes lifelong passive rent. (Section 3)

> **The one sentence that ties the whole module together:** *None of these is a lottery ticket. Each is a slow machine. Own as many small machines as you can, feed them every payday, never eat the seed — and by 40 the machines, not your aching back at the stove, pay your bills.*
